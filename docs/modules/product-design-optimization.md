# Product Design Optimization Module Contract

## Scope

Deferred from Phase 1. This module will consume grounded evidence and product context after retrieval quality gates are stable.

## Inputs

- Retrieved evidence bundles
- Product requirement documents
- Design review notes

## Outputs

- Structured design optimization recommendations
- Traceable evidence-backed rationale
- KPI suggestions for validation

## Dependencies

- Phase 1 retrieval and citation contracts
- Operator review workflow

## KPIs

- Recommendation grounding rate
- Citation coverage rate
- Review acceptance rate
